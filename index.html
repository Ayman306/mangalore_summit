About Us

This is a historic moment in time! For the first-time ever in Mangalore, two of the biggest social media portals representing the serene coastal town of Mangalore in South India have come together with a vision. To nurture and grow the content creator and influencer community in and around the coastal hub of Karnataka.
Unbeknownst to the rest of India, Mangalore city has been quietly yet aggressively producing some of the finest, coolest, and most popular social media influencers and content creators. Names that are well-known all over India like Finance with Sharan have their roots in Mangalore. SaltSnap and Mangalore Meri Jaan felt a strong need for a dedicated forum where the people of Mangalore city could come together, recognize and interact with these popular figures.
